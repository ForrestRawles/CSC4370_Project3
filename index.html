Fifteen Puzzle

The goal of the 15 Puzzle is to un-jumble its 15 squares by repeatedly making moves that slides squares into the empty space.
How quickly can you solve it?

Moves: 0

Remaining Time: 120 seconds



Background Image:

American puzzle author and Mathamatic Sam Loyd is often falsely credited with creating the puzzle; indeed,
Loyd claimed in 1891 unti his death in 1911 that he invented it. The puzzlewas actually created around 1874
by Nayes Palmer Chapman, a postmaster in Canastota, New York.
